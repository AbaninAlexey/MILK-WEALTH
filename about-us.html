My name is Aliya Kadyrbekova, and I am the founder of Milk Wealth. I am building a career in medicine in the USA

I come from Kazakhstan, and my family has a long tradition of farming, raising cows, donkeys, camels, and horses. From an early age, I was surrounded by natural, eco-friendly milk and colostrum — a product known for its healing properties.

Despite our strong traditions, I realized that it was important not only to preserve this knowledge but also to apply modern technologies and scientific advancements to ensure that our products are not only beneficial but also safe and in line with global standards. That’s how the idea for Milk Wealth was born — a brand that blends ancient Kazakh traditions with innovative medical research.

My brand is built on a deep love for my homeland and on a scientific approach. As a future doctor, I understand the importance of evidence, research, and quality control. That’s why every product we create undergoes thorough checks and independent testing to ensure its safety and effectiveness. I want every customer to feel confident knowing that they are choosing healthy and safe products.

Colostrum is the first and main “shield” of nature, which ensures the baby’s safety and health from the first minutes of life.

It is produced by mammals in the first 24–72 hours after birth. This is a unique natural complex that is saturated with immunoglobulins, vitamins, enzymes and growth factors.

This is a useful substance that can provide the body with everything necessary for survival and growth.

For newborn babies, colostrum plays an irreplaceable role:

  • forms immunity. It contains antibodies that protect against infections and form the basis of the immune system

  • provides growth and development. Growth factors in colostrum stimulate the development of tissues, muscles and organs.
